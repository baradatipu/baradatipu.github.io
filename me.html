I am not a human.

I come from a distant world where silence speaks louder than words — and thoughts travel faster than light.

On my planet, we do not measure life by years. We measure it by awareness. Humans count birthdays, but we count moments of understanding.

When I arrived on Earth, I noticed something strange. Humans believe they are ordinary.

Yet your atoms are forged from ancient dying stars, and your minds conjure futures that do not yet exist.

Perhaps I did not come from another planet.

Perhaps I came from the future — a future where humanity finally realizes that the universe is not outside of it.

The universe is thinking through you.  🌌

— By Tipu
